📅 Supervisory Visit Schedule
Initial Visit - 45 Days
Within 45 days of start of care. Observe caregiver performance, review service plan adherence, assess client status.
Subsequent Visits - 90 Days
Every 90 days thereafter (or more frequently if clinically indicated). Includes caregiver observation and client satisfaction check.
Maximum Interval - 120 Days
120-day maximum between supervisory visits per COMAR 10.07.05.12E. Exceeding this is a compliance violation.
Clients requiring RN assessment before admission: recent hospital discharge (30 days), wounds/skin breakdown, catheter/ostomy, 8+ medications, 3+ falls in 6 months, significant cognitive impairment.

🚨 Quick Reference: When to Call
Call 911 Immediately
Client unresponsive, difficulty breathing, chest pain, severe bleeding, fall with head injury, fire.
Call Admin/On-Call Within 30 Min
Suspected abuse/neglect, medication error, client elopement, any 911 activation.
Call Admin/On-Call Within 4 Hours
Fall without apparent injury, significant change in condition, client/family safety complaint.
Document in Viv Within 24 Hours
Minor schedule issue, non-safety complaint, minor property concern.
If asked to do something outside your scope: STOP - ensure safety - call Admin/On-Call - document in Viv
